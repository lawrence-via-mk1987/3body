## Game Summary

Kairos Redeemer is a premium single-player, Christ-centered time-travel JRPG. The player leads Elior and a party of Spirit-gifted companions across wounded ages of history, restoring the Fruit of the Spirit and confronting the Ancient Serpent, whose primary weapon is distortion of truth and identity.

## Product Definition

- Genre: Story-driven JRPG
- Structure: Hub-and-spoke chapter progression
- Presentation: HD-2D or stylized 2.5D
- Combat: ATB-inspired party combat with Dual/Triple Techs
- Platforms: PC and consoles
- Main playtime target: 30-40 hours
- Completionist target: 45-55 hours

## Core Pillars

1. Christ is the center of the setting, narrative, and meaning
2. The Serpent's chief weapon is distortion, not brute force
3. Identity in Christ must be mechanical, not only thematic
4. Fruit and gifts must work together
5. The world is both historical and spiritual
6. Elior is a witness-participant, not a substitute messiah
7. Every era should feel humanly recognizable

## Core Systems

### Exploration
- authored map flow
- visible encounters
- Veiled World overlay
- layer gates
- prayer/rest points
- character-specific traversal utilities

### Combat
- 3 active party members
- ATB/initiative rhythm
- Tech / Dual Tech / Triple Tech
- spiritual damage types
- boss lie mechanics

### Signature System: Lie / Truth
Bosses impose named lies such as:
- "You are alone"
- "You must prove yourself"
- "Your failure defines you"
- "You are not beloved"

Players break lies using:
- Elior truth skills
- party synergy
- relics
- prayer
- Scripture-linked effects
- Fruit-based resistance

### Assurance
Shared spiritual stability meter. High Assurance improves truth resistance and healing; low Assurance makes accusation and deception more dangerous.

### Fruit Restoration
Each restored Fruit grants narrative, combat, and exploration benefits.

## Boss Framework

Every major boss should have:
- one dominant lie
- one era-specific thematic distortion
- at least two phases
- a truth-break vulnerability
- visible environmental transformation after defeat
