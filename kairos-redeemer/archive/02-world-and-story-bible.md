# Kairos Redeemer - World and Story Bible

## The Turning Point in the Concept

The project moved from a broad Christian fantasy premise into a specifically Christ-centered JRPG. The defining correction was:

> The world is not merely healed by virtue restored.  
> It is healed because all creation holds together in Christ.

## Central Identity Truth

The Serpent's deepest lie became:

> "You are outside the Son. You are not beloved. Make a name for yourself."

The answering truth became:

> "I am beloved because I am in the Beloved Son."

The fixed center of all history:

- Incarnation
- Ministry of Yeshua
- Cross
- Resurrection
- Pentecost

These events are never altered by time travel. They are witnessed and interpreted.

## Real-World Historical Inspirations

Each era takes inspiration from recognizable historical patterns:

- Garden of First Light: primal fall and the birth of shame
- Emberfall: postwar Europe and collective grief
- War of Two Crowns: holy wars and national-religious conflict
- Desert of Waiting: exile, refugee endurance, and wilderness faith
- Iron Market: empire, debt systems, extraction, industrial capitalism
- Hollow Temple: religious corruption and performative holiness
- Broken Covenant: court betrayal and social trust collapse
- Arena of Beasts: coliseum logic, authoritarian spectacle, violence as entertainment
- Mirror Citadel: self-invention, consumer identity, and appetite culture
